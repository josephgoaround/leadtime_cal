About LeadTime Intelligence

Fusing maritime expertise with advanced AI to illuminate global supply chains.

LeadTime Intelligence was born from a simple observation: the world's most critical infrastructure—global logistics—is often the least transparent. By combining decades of seafaring experience with cutting-edge graph theory and real-time data processing, we've built a platform that doesn't just calculate distances, but predicts the heartbeat of global trade.

Our mission is to empower logistics professionals with the tools they need to navigate an increasingly complex geopolitical landscape. Whether it's rerouting around the Suez Canal or optimizing a single container's load, we provide the intelligence that moves the world.
