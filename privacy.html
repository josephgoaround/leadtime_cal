Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

1. Data Collection

LeadTime Intelligence ("we", "us", or "our") provides logistics analytics. We collect minimal personal data, primarily through voluntary newsletter subscriptions (email addresses) and technical logs for system optimization.

2. Cookies & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

3. Logistics Analytics Data

Route parameters and cargo configurations entered into our Analyzer or 3D Planner are processed locally or in memory to provide results and are not stored permanently on our servers unless specifically saved by a registered user.

4. GDPR & International Compliance

We respect the privacy rights of users globally. Our cookie consent mechanism ensures users have control over tracking technologies used during their session.
