Our Mission

LeadTime Intelligence was founded to bridge the transparency gap in the global supply chain. By combining data science with deep maritime logistics expertise, we provide tools that help shippers and logistics professionals make better, faster decisions in an increasingly complex world.

Data-Driven Precision

Leveraging data from over 250 global hub ports and established liner shipping routes, our Dijkstra-based algorithm provides precise route simulations that account for real-world constraints.

Sustainable Logistics

We aim to minimize carbon emissions and transportation costs by optimizing routes and improving container load planning, contributing to a more efficient and greener global logistics ecosystem.

Why LeadTime Intelligence?

While many tools simply calculate great-circle distances, we integrate actual maritime arteries, geopolitical risk factors, and real-time port congestion data. As a professional platform, we are committed to providing valuable, reliable insights that empower the next generation of supply chain management.
